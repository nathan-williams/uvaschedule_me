The purpose of this website is to make course scheduling more intuitive at the University of Virginia.
Tell us what courses you plan on taking, and we'll provide you with a fast, straightforward interface to build your schedule.

The data is collected from Lou's List on each request, so all information will be accurate to that site (Lou's List mines every hour).
This site was developed and launched in February 2015; it is currently being maintained.

Happy scheduling.
